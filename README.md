# Customer Churn & Retention Analytics Dashboard
**Business-focused churn analysis combining machine learning, Tableau dashboards, and executive reporting to support data-driven retention decisions.**  

This project identifies early churn risk, explains *why* customers leave, and translates analytics into clear actions for financial and operational stakeholders.

✔ Predictive model with **91.8% ROC-AUC**  
✔ Two stakeholder-ready **Tableau dashboards** (exploratory + executive)  
✔ **PowerPoint business report** written for non-technical decision-makers

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
Customer churn directly impacts revenue stability and customer lifetime value.  
This analysis answers one core question:

**How can churn risk be identified early and translated into actionable business decisions?**

The dataset includes customer demographics, service usage, billing behaviour, and account history.

---

## 🔑 Key Business Insights

- **Declining usage is the strongest early churn signal** — customers disengage before cancelling  
- **Lower charges correlate with higher churn**, indicating weak perceived value  
- Churn risk can be detected **well before contract termination**, enabling proactive retention
